FRESH IN THE STATES

Online Show for bridging the gap between international student and American cultural

ABOUT

Fresh In The States is a cultural exchange show that demonstrates how different culture interact with each other in an entertaining way. In the first season, we introduce two pairs of guest coming from America and China. They spent a day together traveling in the greater Boston area. Through their interactions, we demonstrate cultural differences in values, thoughts, and behaviors between American and Chinese. The show earned more than 50,000 clicks.





PRODUCTION

During the pre-production, me and my friends raised $8,000 budget. The whole group is formed by 30+ members from 10+ different majors and 4 universities, including teams of cinematography, post-production, directing, marketing and public relations.



During the production, we designed our own cartoon figures. In order to draw more attention to our show, we held 3 online promotions. More than 1000 people participant in “vote for your favorite actor”. We shoot 100+ hour footages for the first two episodes and finish all the post-production including sound design, editing, video effect by ourselves. Our team is interviewed by China Digital Video Times.
